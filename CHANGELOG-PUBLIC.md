## üéØ What is Phantom ACO?

Phantom ACO is an **Auto-Checkout service** for Pokemon products. We monitor drops across multiple retailers and automatically checkout items for you using your saved payment and account information.

**How it works:**
1. Login with Discord
2. Submit your payment info and preferences
3. We monitor for Pokemon product drops
4. When products drop, we auto-checkout for you
5. You get your items without the stress!
